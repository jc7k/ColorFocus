# Raw Idea: Accessible Color Palette Replacement

## Feature Description

Replace the color palette with a new accessible 8-color palette optimized for users with visual and cognitive impairments. The new palette includes: Black (#1A1A1A), Brown (#8B4513), Purple (#7B4BAF), Blue (#0066CC), Gray (#808080), Pink (#E75480), Orange (#FF8C00), Yellow (#FFD700). It supports nested subsets (2, 4, 6, or 8 colors) for scalable difficulty and includes labels in English, Traditional Chinese, Vietnamese, and Spanish.

## Key Requirements

- Replace existing 8-color palette with new accessible colors
- New colors: Black (#1A1A1A), Brown (#8B4513), Purple (#7B4BAF), Blue (#0066CC), Gray (#808080), Pink (#E75480), Orange (#FF8C00), Yellow (#FFD700)
- Support nested subsets for difficulty scaling: 2, 4, 6, or 8 colors
- Optimize for users with visual and cognitive impairments
- Include labels in 4 languages: English, Traditional Chinese, Vietnamese, Spanish
